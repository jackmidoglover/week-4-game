Crystal Collector

You are a geologist. You are gathering samples of different crystals for a study. Your research grant specifies how many samples you must collect. However, if you collect more than the specified goal, you will anger the mine operators who determine how many crystals you can retrieve for your study!

How to play:

  1. Look at the Goal.
  2. Click on the crystals below. They will add points to your Score.
  3. Keep clicking on crystals to add points to your score, but try to keep in mind how many points each crystal gives you so you don't go over the goal!
  4. If you go over the goal, you lose.
  5. If you match the exact number of crystals, you win!

Have fun!
